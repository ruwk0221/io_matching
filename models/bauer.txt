# BOOLEAN RULES
Cadherin*= Cadherin
VEGFR*= VEGFR
Integrin*= Integrin
GRB2*= VEGFR
Ras*= GRB2
Raf1*= Ras
MEK1*= Raf1 or Rac1
ERK1*= MEK1
PI3K*= Ras and FAK
Akt*= PI3K and not PTEN
Rac1*= PI3K and not RhoA
GSK3beta*= not Akt
Bad*= not Akt
betacatenin*= not Cadherin and not GSK3beta
FAK*= Integrin
p53*= not Integrin
PTEN*= p53
RhoA*= FAK
ROCK1*= RhoA and not ERK1
Actin*= ROCK1 or Rac1
cyclinD1*= ERK1 and betacatenin
